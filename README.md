# 🎥 Tubetoo

A minimal, secure, and modern desktop application designed to download, convert, and trim YouTube videos with ease. Download full videos or extract specific chapters and ranges in high quality.

---

## ✨ Features

- **High-Quality Downloads**: Download videos in multiple resolutions (up to 4K) or extract high-quality audio tracks.
- **Video Trimmer & Chapter Support**: Automatically fetch YouTube video chapters and download/trim specific segments or chapters instantly.
- **Modern Minimalist UI**: Sleek dark-themed interface with custom smooth glassmorphic modals and progress notifications.
- **Multi-Platform Support**: Available for Windows, macOS, and Linux.
- **Privacy & Security Focused**: The backend logic is secured with V8 bytecode encryption, and the frontend is fully obfuscated to ensure your application runs safely and privately.

---

## 🚀 How to Download & Install

Since this repository is used for hosting release builds, you can download the ready-to-run installation package for your operating system directly from the **Releases** section.

### 📥 Steps to Install:

1. Look at the **Releases** section on the right side of this page and click on the latest version (e.g., **`v1.2`**).
2. Download the package corresponding to your Operating System:
   - **Windows**: Download the `.exe` installer. Run the installer and follow the instructions.
   - **macOS**: Download the `.dmg` file. Open the disk image and drag **Tubetoo** to your *Applications* folder.
   - **Linux**: Download the `.tar.gz` archive. Extract it and run the executable.

---

## 🔒 Security & Privacy Notice

Tubetoo operates entirely locally on your computer. 
- It starts a secure local server (`127.0.0.1`) that is strictly bounded to your localhost, meaning no external devices on your network can access your app.
- All downloads, processing, and configurations are saved locally and are never shared.
